My name is Jaspinder(Jas). I have been doing photography since 2015. I first discovered photography when I went to a drift event and asked to borrow my friends DSLR. I was on the track taking pictures and at the end of the event, posted them on the event page on facebook. Word quickly spread and instantly, people were messaging me saying how much they loved the photos. It was there I discovered my knack for photography.
