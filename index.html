I dont just look to capture a picture, but rather I aim to capture a personality, a scene, a moment in which you can look back and feel that time all over again. Relying more so on the ability to capture the vision rather than create it in post editing. Having a majority of my experiance in car photography, I have since branched out to portraits as well as nature and began to even look at more abstract ideas involving long exposure and even creating images that dont really have a meaning but rather a sense of comfort. I look forward to continue growing my skill but in the mean time here is some of my work below!
